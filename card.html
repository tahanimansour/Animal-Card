Cats

Cats have lack taste receptors for sweetness. Cats cannot taste sweet flavors because they don't have the necessary taste buds for detecting sugars. This is due to their evolution as obligate carnivores, relying primarily on proteins rather than carbohydrates in their diet.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Length:45cm-60cm
  • Average Lifespan: 12years-18years
  • Habitat: variety of natural habitats, including urban, rural, forest, and desert environments.

Cats are small carnivorous mammals belonging to the Felidae family. They are common domestic pets characterized by their independent nature, flexible body shape, round ears, and long tail. Cats are known for their hunting ability, aided by their sharp senses and agility, and they inhabit various natural habitats worldwide. They make enjoyable and preferred companions for many, providing numerous psychological and health benefits to their owners.
